Accommodation

University Accomodation

A limited number of rooms are available at Edinburgh Napier University’s Orwell Terrace apartments. These are centrally located, with bars, restaurants and supermarket on the doorstep and just a 15-minute walk to the city centre, and 10-minute walk from Haymarket train station which links with the tram service to Edinburgh Airport.
All apartments feature individual lockable rooms with small double (4ft) beds & private en-suite bathrooms, plus a fully equipped communal kitchen/lounge area with flat screen television. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout and we provide all bed linen, bath & hand towels. More information can be found here.

The rooms can be booked for single occupancy (at £49 per person per night) or for double occupancy (at £59 per night per room). You can book a room by contacting vacation.lets@napier.ac.uk. When booking accommodation, please quote ‘8EV2023’ and provide evidence of your conference booking (eg receipt, booking reference number).

Please be aware that there are only a few rooms available.
