Welcome to the 8th International Electric Vehicle Conference


Dear Industry Partners and Colleagues,

We are delighted to invite you to participate in the 8th International Electric Vehicle Conference, hosted by Edinburgh Napier University.
The 8th International Electric Vehicle Conference aims to provide a scientific venue for sharing ideas and experiences on topics related to electromobility.
The world is rapidly changing towards environmentally friendly technological solutions and energy resources and in this endeavor, the electromobility research community will play a vital role.
Equality, diversity and inclusion are at the heart of the conferenceâ€™s values and we particularly encourage the participation of researchers from under-represented groups.
Thank you for your consideration and we look forward to welcoming you in June 2023.
On behalf of the Programme and Local Organising Committees.

Dr Stathis Tingas
General Chair of the Local Organising Committee of the 8th International Electric Vehicle Conference
